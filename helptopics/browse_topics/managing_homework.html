Mastering your homework workflow

Efficiently add, edit, and complete your homework assignments.

Keeping track of multiple assignments can be overwhelming. Our app simplifies this process so you can focus on learning.

Follow these steps to ensure you never miss a deadline again.

check_circle Key Features

  • Adding Homework: Tap the '+' button, select 'Homework', and fill in the details.
  • Prioritizing: Use the priority flag (High, Medium, Low) to sort your tasks.
  • Completing: Swipe right on a task to mark it as done and celebrate your progress!

Why it matters

Organizing assignments by priority helps you tackle the most urgent tasks first, reducing last-minute stress.
