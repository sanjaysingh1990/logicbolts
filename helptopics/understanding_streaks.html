Understanding Streaks

What are streaks and how do they work?

local_fire_department How it works

A streak represents the number of consecutive days you have completed at least one task or study session.

  • Daily Goal: Complete 1 task every 24 hours.
  • Reset: If you miss a day, the streak counter resets to zero. Consistency is key!
  • Rewards: Reach milestones like 7, 30, or 100 days to unlock exclusive trophies.
