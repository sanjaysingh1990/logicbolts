Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

ClassMate+ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

info Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Profile information (name, avatar - optional)

Academic Data

  • Homework assignments and deadlines
  • Exam schedules and study plans
  • Subject and class information
  • Achievement and statistics data

Usage Information

  • App usage analytics (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Device information and app preferences

how_to_reg How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
  • Sync your data across devices via cloud backup
  • Send notifications about upcoming deadlines and exams
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

cloud_sync Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption:

  • Local Storage: Data is stored locally on your device using encrypted databases
  • Cloud Backup: Optional cloud sync uses Firebase/Supabase with end-to-end encryption
  • Authentication: Secure authentication via Firebase Auth

share Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Firebase, Supabase for backend services (bound by strict privacy agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing

admin_panel_settings Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and non-essential data collection

child_care Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to request deletion.

notifications Notifications

We send push notifications to remind you about upcoming deadlines and exams. You can control notification preferences in the app settings or your device settings at any time.

update Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
